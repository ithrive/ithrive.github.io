A multi-disciplinary approach

ithrive is an integrated massage and movement therapy practice. Laura draws from a diverse range of innovative and effective methods, ensuring that the treatment you receive is as unique as you are.

JEMS®

JEMS® is a movement enhancement approach which targets the body’s efficiency of motion, and addresses physical performance as well as injury prevention and rehabilitation.

“The JEMS mission is to enable people of all backgrounds, ages and levels of physical ability to learn the art of working with their body instead of against it, to have confidence in their bodies, and to reclaim and enjoy the activities that matter most to them.” - JEMS creator Joanne Elphinston

OOV®

The Oov® is both a diagnostic and a training tool. It’s a supportive device that facilitates decompression and optimal alignment of the spine while at the same time providing a multi-planar challenge to stability. Oov training promotes rapid change by providing instant feedback.

Be Activated

This system uses muscle strength tests to asses and then guide a form of myofascial release that aims to restore an expansive, centre-out movement strategy. It addresses breathing restrictions and promotes optimal interaction between diaphragm, psoas and gluteal muscles.

Pilates

Promotes balance in the body through development of core strength and flexibility with an emphasis on healthy spine mechanics.

Remedial Massage

Massage and mobilisation of soft tissues of the body, i.e. muscles, fascia, tendons, ligaments and joints. May include techniques such as trigger point therapy, myofascial release, joint mobilisation and muscle energy technique.
