A multi-disciplinary approach

ithrive is an integrated massage and movement therapy practice. Laura draws from a diverse range of innovative and effective methods, ensuring that the treatment you receive is as unique as you are.

JEMS®

JEMS® is a movement enhancement approach built on a foundation of neuroscience, biomechanics, psychology and rehabilitation research.

“The JEMS mission is to enable people of all backgrounds, ages and levels of physical ability to learn the art of working with their body instead of against it, to have confidence in their bodies, and to reclaim and enjoy the activities that matter most to them.” - JEMS creator Joanne Elphinston

OOV®

The Oov® is both a diagnostic and a training tool. It’s a supportive device that facilitates decompression and optimal alignment of the spine while at the same time providing a multi-planar challenge to stability. The oov promotes rapid neuromuscular change by providing instant feedback.

Be Activated

This system uses muscle strength tests to assess and then guide a form of myofascial release that aims to restore an expansive, centre-out movement strategy. It addresses breathing restrictions and promotes optimal interaction between diaphragm, psoas and gluteal muscles.

Remedial Massage
