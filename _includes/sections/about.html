About Laura

Laura is a massage and movement specialist who brings a unique breadth of knowledge and experience to the practice. With 12 years experience as a soft tissue therapist, she combines an intuitive touch with expertise in several movement disciplines. After initially training in massage therapy and then Pilates, Laura was quick to recognise the enhanced benefit of combining hands on therapy and movement work. Since then, she has been dedicated to continually expanding her knowledge in both domains - developing a uniquely diverse skill-set including JEMS, Oov training and the Be Activated technique. As such, Laura can often bring new insight and progress to people with chronic issues that have been resistant to a more conventional approach.
